Common Questions

Pricing & Package FAQs

Get answers to common questions about our pricing and service packages.

Are there any setup fees or long-term contracts?

We do not charge setup fees for most of our standard service packages, and we offer flexible month-to-month options with no long-term commitment required. For custom enterprise solutions, there may be a one-time onboarding fee depending on the complexity of implementation, which we'll clearly outline in your quote.

Can I upgrade or downgrade my plan later?

Yes, you can change your service plan at any time with proper notice (typically 14 days before the next billing cycle). We understand that business needs fluctuate, and our flexible approach allows you to scale services up during busy periods and reduce them when needed.

Do you offer discounts for multiple services?

Yes, we offer bundle discounts when you subscribe to multiple services. The discount typically ranges from 10-20% depending on the combination of services selected. For a personalized quote on service bundles, please contact our sales team.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept major credit cards, ACH transfers, and electronic invoicing through platforms like QuickBooks and FreshBooks. For enterprise clients, we can also set up specialized payment arrangements. All payments are processed securely through industry-standard encryption.

What if I need more hours or services than my plan includes?

Additional hours or services can be purchased as needed. For hourly-based services like virtual assistance, you can purchase hours at your plan's standard hourly rate. If you consistently need more hours, we recommend upgrading to the next service tier for better rates.

Is there a money-back guarantee?

We offer a satisfaction guarantee on all our services. If you're not satisfied with the quality of our work, we'll work with you to resolve the issue or provide appropriate compensation. For most services, we offer a 14-day trial period during which you can cancel for a full refund if not completely satisfied.
