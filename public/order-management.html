Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions about our order management services.

How quickly can you start managing our orders?

We can typically begin managing your orders within 1-2 weeks after completing our onboarding process. This includes system integration, training our team on your specific requirements, and establishing communication protocols.

How do you ensure order accuracy?

We implement a multi-step verification process for every order. This includes initial validation upon receipt, cross-checking during processing, and final review before confirmation. Our process-oriented approach and trained staff ensure we maintain a 99.8% accuracy rate.

Can you handle international orders and shipping?

Yes, we have extensive experience managing international orders. Our team understands the complexities of international shipping, including customs documentation, duty calculations, and country-specific regulations. We work with all major global carriers to ensure smooth delivery worldwide.

What types of reporting do you provide?

We provide comprehensive reporting including daily order summaries, weekly processing metrics, monthly performance analytics, and custom reports tailored to your specific needs. All reports include actionable insights to help improve your order management process continuously.

How are customer service inquiries handled?

Our team can manage customer service inquiries related to orders via email, phone, or your preferred communication channel. We respond promptly to questions about order status, shipping updates, and other common inquiries, escalating more complex issues to your team when necessary.
