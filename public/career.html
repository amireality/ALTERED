Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions about careers at Altered.

What is the application process like?

Our application process typically involves four steps: (1) Online application submission, (2) Initial screening call, (3) Skills assessment relevant to the position, and (4) Final interview with the team lead or manager. The process usually takes 2-3 weeks from application to offer.

Does Altered offer remote work opportunities?

Yes, many of our positions are remote or offer flexible work arrangements. Each job posting will specify whether the position is remote, in-office, or hybrid. We believe in creating a work environment that allows our team members to deliver their best work while maintaining work-life balance.

What training and development opportunities are available?

We offer comprehensive onboarding for all new hires, ongoing skill development through our learning platform, mentorship programs, and regular workshops and webinars. We also provide financial assistance for relevant professional certifications and continuing education.

How does Altered support career growth?

We believe in promoting from within and helping our team members advance their careers. We provide clear career pathways, regular performance reviews, growth-focused feedback, and opportunities to work on cross-functional projects to develop new skills. Many of our team leads and managers started in entry-level positions.

What is the company culture like?

Our culture is built on collaboration, respect, and continuous improvement. We value open communication, celebrate diversity of thought, and encourage innovation. While we maintain a professional environment, we also foster a supportive and friendly atmosphere where team members feel valued and heard.
