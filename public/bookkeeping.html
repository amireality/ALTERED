Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions about our bookkeeping services.

How quickly can you start managing our books?

We can typically begin managing your bookkeeping within 1-2 weeks after our initial consultation. This includes setting up your accounts, configuring software integrations, and establishing data transfer procedures.

What if I need to switch from another bookkeeping service?

We offer a smooth transition process, including a thorough review of your current books, reconciliation of accounts, and seamless data migration. We'll work with you to ensure continuity and accuracy during the transition period.

How do you ensure the security of my financial data?

We implement bank-level security measures including data encryption, secure access controls, regular security audits, and strict confidentiality protocols. All our bookkeepers sign NDAs, and we comply with industry standards for financial data protection.

What types of financial reports will I receive?

Our standard reporting includes monthly profit and loss statements, balance sheets, cash flow reports, accounts receivable/payable aging reports, and customized financial dashboards. We can also create specialized reports based on your specific business needs.

Do you work with businesses in specific industries?

Yes, we have experience with a wide range of industries including retail, e-commerce, professional services, healthcare, nonprofits, construction, and manufacturing. Our team understands industry-specific accounting requirements and best practices.
